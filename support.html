Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you don't receive an email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
How can I download my transcript?
You can request an official transcript from the Registrar's Office. Visit their office in Building B, Room 202, or call (555) 123-4567 to request one.
What is the late fee policy?
Late fees are assessed at $50 per day after the due date. To avoid late fees, ensure your payment is received by the deadline. Financial aid may cover some fees.
How do I request course materials?
Contact your instructor directly through the course portal, or visit the bookstore in the Student Center. Some materials are available for free in the library.
Can I change my course schedule?
Course changes can be made through the portal up to 2 weeks after the semester starts. After that period, you'll need approval from your academic advisor and the department.
How do I apply for a refund?
Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of payment. Visit the Finance Office in Building C, Room 105 with your payment receipt and ID.
